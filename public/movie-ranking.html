The Best Movies According to Lenk

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

I enjoyed the first two parts of The Godfather's saga but if i had to pick one, that will be the first part. Before watching it, i heard people saying incredible things about it and i was curious about why, so i give it a try and damn, it did not dissapoint. Pretty good movie, very good acting. Just perfect.

Back to the Future Trilogy

I'm unable to pick one so i will pick the whole trilogy (I like seeing them as a whole movie). Some things maybe have aged poorly, like the CGI, but despite that it's a very entertaining adventure. This trilogy it's arguably one of the Sci-fi related things out there.

Rango

A very well animated western movie that wasn't appreciated in it's time. A movie about of finding yourself on this world full of uncertainty. Recommend to everyone even if your not a fan of westerns.
